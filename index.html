About

I am a software engineer with a Bachelor of Arts in Computer Science from Middlebury College, Class of 2024. My primary interests lie in AI, web development, and security systems, with a growing interest in emerging technologies. My liberal arts background complements my technical skills, enabling me to approach problems with both analytical and creative mindsets. I am seeking opportunities to contribute to innovative projects that push the boundaries of technology while making a positive impact.
